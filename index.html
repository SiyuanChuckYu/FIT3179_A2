Australia Economic Snapshot 2025

Choropleth Map: Unemployment by State/Territory (Aug 2025)

Each shade represents the August 2025 unemployment rate by state/territory. Hover to see the exact value and state name; use the legend to interpret the colour scale.

Source: ABS | TopoJSON Natural Earth

Racing Bar: Unemployment (1978–Present)

Bars race through time to show how each state’s unemployment rate changes year by year. Use the filter to limit states and hover to see precise values.

Source: ABS (averaged monthly)

Zoomable Sunburst: GVA by Industry (Chain volume, Jun 2024)

Hierarchical view of Gross Value Added by industry, measured in chain‑volume (A$ millions). This idiom shows the whole‑to‑parts structure: larger arcs indicate larger economic contribution. Click an arc to drill into sub‑industries; click the centre to zoom out. Insight: quickly see which industries (and sub‑industries) drive Australia’s GVA and how the mix is composed.

Source: ABS — GVA by Industry (chain volume)

Overview & Detail: Household Spending (2013–2024)

Stacked area of annual household spending by state (A$ millions). The top panel shows each state’s contribution over time; the bottom overview (with brush) filters the period. This idiom reveals trend strength and composition: identify growth periods, compare states’ shares, and see when contribution shifts occur.

Source: ABS — Australian System of National Accounts 2023–24

Bump: Weekly Income Rank by State (2007–2020)

Bump chart ranking states by mean weekly household income (A$ per household). Lines trace rank over years; use the slider to choose the Top N and click legend to focus states. This idiom highlights relative standing and cross‑overs—who leads, who converges, and when rankings change.

Source: ABS — Household income and wealth
